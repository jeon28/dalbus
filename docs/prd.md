# Share King (쉐어킹) - PRD (Product Requirements Document)

## Overview
"Share King" is a platform for sharing premium OTT/Service subscriptions (starting with Tidal) to reduce costs for individual users. It aims to provide a reliable, automated (eventually), and user-friendly experience similar to "Pickle Plus".

## Objectives
- Provide a cost-effective way for users to access premium services.
- Automate the account sharing process (matching, notification).
- Ensure secure transactions through verified identities and payment systems.

## Key Features
1. **Landing/Home**: List of available services (Tidal, etc.) with pricing and benefits.
2. **Subscription Flow**:
   - Choose a service.
   - Login/Signup (via PortOne authentication).
   - Enter payment info (via PortOne).
   - Agree to terms.
3. **My Page**:
   - View active subscriptions.
   - Access account credentials (ID/PW) for shared services.
   - Check subscription periods.
4. **Admin Dashboard**:
   - View order list and payment status.
   - Manually/Automatically assign account credentials to users.
   - Update service categories.

## UX/UI Principles
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for mobile screens but responsive to desktop.
- **Clean & Premium**: High-quality design with smooth transitions (Pickle Plus style).
- **Simplicity**: Minimal steps to complete a subscription.
